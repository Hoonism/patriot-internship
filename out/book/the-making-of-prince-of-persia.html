The Making of Prince of Persia

The Making of Prince of Persia

Jordan Mechner

A pioneer in game design takes us through the development of one of the most innovative games of all time.

Originally released in 1989, Prince of Persia stunned players with its fluid animation and immersive storytelling. These illustrated journals provide a unique window into the creative process of game development in the 1980s.
